Our Process

1. Discovery & Planning

We kick things off with a stakeholder alignment and goal setting meeting, which helps us to find the best solution for your needs.

Read more

2. Crafting The Experience

We start by creating personas and user stories and then move into initial sketches and wireframes.

Read more

3. Design

We take all the info that was gathered from the Discovery phase, the personas, and the wireframes to create a mockup for your initial review.

Read more

4. Build

This is where everything comes together to realize your vision. We run a number of sprints to go from proof of concept to minimum viable product to production-ready release.

Read more

5. Tests & Tweaks

Before moving on to production it is essential to test the final product in order to adjust and optimize it again.

Read more

6. Going Live

We run one final QA round just to make sure everything is functioning. We coordinate together to schedule a launch date and time.
